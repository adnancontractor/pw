Projects & Publications

Signal Processing · Geophysics

Classification Models for Seismic Waveform Analysis

Trained classification models to detect waveform disruptions in seismic time series with a deliberate focus on ultra-low-frequency sensitivity. The work emphasized separating true geophysical structure from instrumentation drift and ambient noise using spectral features, windowed statistics, and robust decision thresholds calibrated to rare-event detection rather than average accuracy.

Market Microstructure · Crypto

Cryptocurrency Arbitrage Strategies

Explored liquidity-based arbitrage across centralized and decentralized venues, including triangular paths and CEX/DEX dislocations where the limiting factor is execution, not quote visibility. The analysis treated arbitrage as a constrained optimization problem under stochastic depth, fees, latency, and slippage, with special attention to when "paper spreads" fail to survive realistic fill assumptions.

Energy Systems · Applied Physics

Directed Thermionic Energy Conversion

Studied thermionic energy conversion in device-level settings where heat removal is the dominant constraint. The project modeled electron emission efficiency alongside cooling pathways and circuit integration, aiming to identify regimes where thermionic generators can be stabilized and made practically useful inside electrical systems rather than treated as isolated lab-scale components.

Machine Learning · Theory

Energy Based Adaptive Routing in Neural Networks

Investigated architectures that route computation adaptively using an energy-based criterion rather than a fixed forward pass. The core idea is to couple representation learning with a routing policy that activates only the subnetworks needed for a given input, pushing toward conditional computation that is both efficient and interpretable under a principled objective.

Quantitative Finance · Derivatives

Exotic Payoff Design: Liquidity Sensitive Stochastic Models

Designed exotic payoff structures that explicitly encode liquidity conditions into valuation and risk, rather than treating execution as an external post-processing step. The work modeled liquidity as a latent or observed state variable interacting with price dynamics, motivating payoffs whose realized performance depends on spread, depth, and market impact regimes.

Complex Systems · Trading

Hierarchical Complexity in Multi-Agent Trading Systems

Studied market behavior as an emergent phenomenon arising from interacting agents operating at multiple time scales. The project focused on hierarchical control and delegation: low-level reactive execution policies interacting with higher-level strategic objectives, and how feedback loops, competition, and information constraints can create non-linear dynamics and regime-like shifts.

Medical Imaging · Deep Learning

Image Resolution of HCC Ultrasound Scans Using Generative Adversarial Networks

Developed an adversarial-learning approach to enhance ultrasound image resolution for hepatocellular carcinoma analysis. The emphasis was on preserving clinically meaningful texture and boundary structure while reducing artifact amplification, positioning GAN-based super-resolution as a practical complement to standard enhancement pipelines.

Plasma Physics · Nuclear Medicine

Modeling of Yttrium-90 and Technetium-99m Production Using Plasma-Based Synthesis

Applied computational physics methods to model plasma-based synthesis routes for medically relevant isotopes, including Y-90 and Tc-99m. The work focused on yield, stability, and energy-efficiency constraints in the synthesis pathway, with motivation tied to reliable isotope production for diagnostics and targeted radiotherapy workflows.

Generative Models · Asset Management

Multi-modal Generative Models for Portfolio Optimization

Built multi-modal generative models intended to support portfolio optimization when historical data is sparse or structurally non-stationary. The approach combines heterogeneous information sources to generate coherent scenario distributions, enabling stress testing, sensitivity analysis, and robust optimization under distributional uncertainty.

MRI · Medical Imaging

Neural Style Transfer for PC-MRI

Explored neural style transfer to improve the realism and diversity of synthetic PC-MRI data used for segmentation and classification tasks. The goal was to better match scanner- and patient-specific appearance statistics while preserving flow-relevant structure, improving downstream model generalization without requiring costly new labels.

Time Series · Generative Finance

Portfolio Rebalancing with Flow-Based Generative Models

Developed flow-based generative models for synthetic time-series generation across multiple asset classes, using the generated scenarios to supply and stress portfolio rebalancing logic. The project emphasized tail behavior, cross-asset dependency structure, and regime sensitivity so that portfolio tools can be evaluated under realistic distribution shifts rather than a single historical path.

Control Theory · Fusion

Predicting What Happens in a Tokamak with Stochastic Predictive Control

Modeled tokamak behavior through stochastic predictive control, treating regime transitions and measurement noise as first-class constraints. The project framed prediction and stabilization as a receding-horizon control problem, aimed at producing policies that remain robust under partial observability and rapidly changing plasma dynamics.

Macroeconomics · Time Series

Regime Shift Modeling - Recession Risk

Built a regime-shift framework over macro indicators in the FRED database to identify and characterize recession risk as a transition problem rather than a point forecast. The work emphasized state inference, persistence, and leading indicators that behave differently across regimes, with the goal of making "recession probability" a structured, testable object.

Algorithms · Mechanism Design

Specialized Search Algorithms for Combinatorial Auctions

Designed and analyzed specialized search procedures for combinatorial auction winner determination under tight computational budgets. The emphasis was on structure-aware branching and pruning, approximation-aware heuristics, and search strategies that exploit realistic bid languages, targeting practical tractability without sacrificing the expressive power needed in real settings.

Derivatives · Stochastic Processes

Stochastic Correlation in Multi Asset Exotic Options

Studied multi-asset exotic options where correlation is itself stochastic and materially impacts valuation and hedging error. The project focused on modeling correlation as a state variable with its own dynamics, examining how correlation regimes propagate into payoff distribution tails and create risk that cannot be hedged by delta exposure alone.

Mathematics · Spectral Graph Theory

Uniformization Problems in Spectral Graph Theory

Explored uniformization-type questions on combinatorial and quantum graphs, with a particular focus on estimating extremal adjacency spectral gaps. The work investigated double-kite graph families as a testbed for bounding gap behavior, combining analytical reasoning with computational estimation to map where "minimum gap" constructions plausibly live.
